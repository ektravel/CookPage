    The site for all your Food and Drink related needs

    Welcome to EDC, your friendly search engine for food and drinks. Whether you feel like eating out, having a drink in the city, or cooking a delicious meal at home, EDC will help you find what you are looking for. Navigate to the Eat page to explore all of the different kinds of restaurants in your city. Visit the Drink page to access information on local bars. And if you want to impress your family and friends with your cooking skills, check out the Cook page for creative recipes. We created EDC to help you review and narrow down local eating and drinking options. Finding the right place can be hard and this site makes it easier to find the answer.
